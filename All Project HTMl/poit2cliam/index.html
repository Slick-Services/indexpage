Frequently Asked Question ?

  • 01 What is Points2Claim?

    Points2Claim conducts surveys and polls on the Internet about varying topics like, politics, public affairs, products, brands and other topics of general interest.

  • 02 How long is a typical Points2Claim survey?

    Most Points2Claim surveys are very short and should only take you about five to ten minutes to complete. A typical survey is only about 20 to 30 fairly short questions in total. Occasionally, there will be longer surveys or surveys with more complex questions.

  • 03 What types of data are collected?

    Points2Claim is a community to help understand how people use the internet. What websites they visit, and which apps they use. We collect generic data and never any personal data is shared with the client without your prior consent.

  • 04 For what purposes is the data collected by Points2Claim used?

    Data from Points2Claim surveys are used by news media, public affairs groups, institutions, political campaigns, companies and marketing agencies, to reveal and analyse public opinion.
